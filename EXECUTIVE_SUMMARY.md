## Bottom Line

**Mem0** is the most mature, research-backed, and production-ready with the largest community (43K stars) but lacks enterprise features and public forum presence.

**Memori** has an innovative interceptor-based architecture with extensive framework integrations but critically lacks research validation, benchmarks, and has a smaller community (5.6K stars).

**Supermemory** has performance claims and modern architecture but critically lacks research validation and comprehensive documentation.

**All three** need standardization, better benchmarking, research validation (Memori & Supermemory), and stronger community engagement to reach their full potential.

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Mem0**: Research-backed, largest community, production-proven
- **Memori**: Interceptor-based, extensive integrations, flexible SQL storage
- **Supermemory**: Performance claims, modern architecture, multi-modal support
